La Puerta del Sol and the surrounding area may be a major attraction for tourists, but don't let that put you off it! Both Spaniards and people coming from abroad to live in Madrid call this area their home, and with so much to do you can see why. You're never short of bars, restaurants, theatres... and all at a stone's throw from your door. While the prices may not be cheap, the views and convenience certainly make up for it. Fans of churros will love the nearby churrerías. Fans of history will love the town hall. And fans of Tinder and Bumble will love the number of matches they get in this historic centre.
